We are P.E.T Team

Plastic Bottle Corporation was established in 1981. The company produces stock, custom and customized
plastic bottles, jugs and jars in a wide variety of materials, shapes and sizes. We produce in the United States and Canada and are experienced and knowledgeable. We aggressively seek new market and service opportunities.

The company owns nearly 400 sets of blow molds and is capable of producing containers ranging in size from
1 ounce to 4 liters. We produce containers for automotive additives and oils, auto/RV/marine/cycle products , household, sanitary maintenance and industrial products, personal care, pet and lawn care products.
Containers are produced in HDPE, LDPE, MDPE, PP, OPET, PETG and PVC.
